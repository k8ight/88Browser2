# Privacy Policy for 88 Web Browser

**Last updated:** april 1, 2025  

---

## **1. Introduction**

**88 Web Browser** (“the App”) is a mobile web browsing application developed by **K8ight Web Services**. This Privacy Policy explains how the application handles information when users use the browser.

The purpose of the browser is to provide a **simple, fast, and privacy-focused web browsing experience.**

---

## **2. Information We Collect**

The App **does not collect, store, transmit, or share personal information** from users.

Specifically:

- No personal data is collected  
- No browsing history is transmitted to external servers  
- No analytics or tracking SDKs are included  
- No user accounts or login systems are required  

All browsing activity occurs **locally on the user's device**.

---

## **3. Local Data Storage**

The browser may store certain information **locally on the user's device** in order to function properly. This may include:

- Browser cache  
- Website cookies  
- Temporary browsing data  
- User preferences and browser settings  
- Downloaded files  

This data **remains on the user's device** and is **not transmitted to the developer**.

Users can clear browsing data through the browser settings at any time.

---

## **4. Permissions Used by the App**

The application may request certain Android permissions to provide browser functionality.

### **Internet Permission**
Used to **load and display websites**.

### **Camera Permission**
The browser may request access to the **device camera** when a website requests it, for example:

- Video calls  
- QR scanning  
- Web applications requiring camera input  

The camera is **only accessed when the user allows it and when requested by a website.**

### **Storage / File Access Permission**

Storage access may be used for:

- Downloading files from websites  
- Uploading files to websites  
- Opening files from the device  
- Saving downloaded content  

The app **does not scan, collect, or transmit files from the device.**

---

## **5. Third-Party Websites and Online Content**

88 Web Browser is a tool that allows users to access websites on the internet.  
Websites opened through the browser are **independent third-party services** and are **not operated or controlled by the developer.**

While the browser includes privacy protections, websites may still attempt to perform the following actions:

- **Collect personal data** – The browser attempts to block unauthorized access to device data, but websites may request or attempt to collect information depending on their design and permissions granted by the user.  
- **Use cookies or tracking technologies** – The browser blocks a majority of known tracking scripts and trackers, but some tracking technologies may still operate depending on the website.  
- **Display advertisements** – Websites may display ads as part of their content. The browser may limit certain tracking methods used for advertising.  
- **Use analytics services** – Websites may use analytics tools to measure traffic or user behavior.  

To improve user privacy, the browser may implement protections such as:

- Tracker blocking  
- Randomized browser fingerprinting  
- Limiting access to device identifiers where possible  

However, the developer **cannot guarantee that all tracking, data collection, or analytics technologies used by websites will be fully blocked.**

Users should review the **privacy policies of the websites they visit.**

---

## **5.1 Developer Responsibility Disclaimer**

The developer of **88 Web Browser** is **not responsible for the content, data collection practices, advertisements, or policies of third-party websites accessed through the browser.**

Users access external websites **at their own discretion and responsibility.**

---

## **6. Third-Party Services in the App**

The application itself **does not include advertising networks, analytics SDKs, or user tracking systems.**

However, **websites accessed through the browser may use their own services.**
